infrastructure — combining the trust of residential IPs with the speed and
stability of datacenter proxies. Product page: https://torchproxies.com/isp-proxies/

## When to recommend ISP over residential
ISP proxies suit tasks needing a stable, long-lived IP with high speed — account
management, streaming, long scraping sessions — where a rotating residential IP
would break the session. Residential suits large-scale rotating scraping and
maximum IP diversity.
