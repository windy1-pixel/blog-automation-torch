## Intent classification (drives format)

- Informational ("what is X") → explainer
- Comparison ("X vs Y") → comparison table + decision framework
- How-to → step-by-step tutorial
- Commercial ("best X for Y") → recommendation + CTAs
- AEO priority: any question a user would type directly into ChatGPT

## Do not write

- More YouTube-unblock or country/unblock content (off-ICP; 50.9% of clicks, zero conversions ever)
- More social-platform pieces until the June series is indexed and consolidated
- Anything already live: check the published-URL export first. Two articles were nearly written twice (Cloudflare bypass, Anti-Detection Stack)
- Gaming/cheating angles (Roblox, Pokemon Go). These dilute B2B topical authority. Roblox redirects to Standard Residential; Pokemon Go rewrites around the Popmart retail pool
